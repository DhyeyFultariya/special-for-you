March 8 · International Women's Day

She
is.

— and that has always been enough —

for every woman

01

She carried the world
in silence, and still
showed up smiling.

That is not weakness. That has always been extraordinary strength.

02

Every version of her —
soft, fierce, broken, whole —
was always valid.

She never needed to earn the right to simply be herself.

03

She built things.
She healed things.
She held things together.

Often without anyone noticing. Often without asking for credit.

04

She is not too much.
She was never
too anything.

The world just wasn't always ready for someone as real as her.

Here's to
every woman
who ever bloomed
in the dark.

you were seen  ·  you were felt  ·  you mattered

with deep respect & love

Happy
Women's Day.

To the mothers, daughters, sisters, friends.
To the ones who fought quietly.
To the ones still finding their voice.
To every woman — everywhere.
This day belongs to you.

— with love 🌸
